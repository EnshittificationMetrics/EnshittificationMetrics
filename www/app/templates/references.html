The Four Stages of the Downward Enshittification Spiral:
- Stage 1 - Attraction - great UX; innovation and features
- Stage 2 - Monetization - introduce ads, premium features, subscription models; increased data collection
- Stage 3 - Exploitation - algorithms tweaked for revenue, terms for creators less favorable, intrusive ads, paywalls
- Stage 4 - Exodus - dissatisfied users, negative reviews, migration to alternatives, platform doubles down on profit maximization

Enshittification related words/concepts:

- Anti-enshittification (noun) - Efforts or measures taken to prevent or reverse the degradation of a platform or service, aiming to maintain or improve user experience by avoiding profit-driven changes that negatively impact it.
- Disenshittify (verb) - Process of reversing the degradation of a platform or service by removing or reducing the profit-driven changes that negatively impact user experience.
- Enshittening (noun) - Process by which a tech platform degrades over time due to prioritizing profit over user experience.
- Enshittification (noun) - Coined by Cory Doctorow; transformation of a tech platform from a user-centric service to a profit-centric one.
- Enshittificatory (adjective) - Actions or processes that contribute to the degradation of a platform or service through increasing commercialization.
- Enshittifiers (noun) - Entities or individuals responsible for enshittification.
- Enshittifies (verb) - Actions or processes that degrade a platform or service.
- Enshittify (verb) - Process of degrading a platform or service by implementing profit-driven changes that negatively impact user experience.
- Enshittifying (verb) - Ongoing process of a platform or service progressively degrading in quality.
- Enshittocene (noun) - Era characterized by the widespread degradation of digital platforms and services.
- Platform Capitalism (noun) - A kind of capitalism centered on data, used to organize relationships between buyers, sellers, and goods. - Surveillance Capitalism (noun) - Economic system commodifying personal data for profit; collecting, analyzing, and selling user data to third parties (generally without consent) to predict and influence behavior for commercial gain.
- Terminal Enshittification (noun) - Final stage of a platform's decline; eventual collapse or irrelevance.
